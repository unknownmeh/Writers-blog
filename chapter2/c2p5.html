Chapter II · Part V

A Sudden
Silence

"The Distance Between Messages"

Lucifer · Aurora Vista · Victoria · Evervale
The Mood
🌧

The town of Aurora Vista was cloaked in a gentle, misty rain as evening fell — the rhythmic tapping of droplets against the window echoing the somber mood inside Lucifer's apartment. For two days, a disconcerting silence had settled between him and Victoria, disrupting the lively cadence of their usual conversations.

In Evervale, Victoria sat in her cozy studio, gazing out at the town's picturesque streets. She noticed the shift immediately — their exchanges had become terse and sporadic, filled with lengthy pauses. Hoping to reignite their usual spark, she reached for her phone.

The Distance
📱
Victoria Hey Luc, hope you're doing okay. Miss our talks. Any thoughts on that new painting idea? Could use your brilliant mind.
⏱ Hours pass…
Lucifer Hey, V. Just busy with stuff. Haven't had much time to think about art. Your idea sounds good, though.
Victoria Is everything alright? You've seemed really distant lately. You know you can talk to me about anything, right?
⏱ Even longer this time…
Lucifer Just some personal stuff. It's been rough. Don't really want to get into it.
Victoria I'm here if you need to talk, Luc. Even if it's just to vent — I miss our chats and your jokes. It feels weird without them.
Lucifer Thanks, V. I appreciate it. Just need some time. Sorry if I've been distant. Hope you're doing well.
Victoria I understand. Take all the time you need. Just know I'm here, okay? Evervale isn't the same without our crazy chats.
⏱ An hour later…
Victoria Luc, I miss our banter. It's like my creative muse took a vacation without telling me. Everything okay?
Lucifer Yeah, just a lot on my plate. Miss our talks too. Wish I could say more, but things are complicated right now.
Lucifer I really miss our talks too, Victoria. Wish things were different.
⏱ A long moment…
Victoria Me too, Luc. Take care of yourself, okay? We'll get through this.
· · · · ·

The messages had become a lifeline — each one a thread holding their friendship together. But the once-vibrant conversation had dwindled, leaving them both adrift in a sea of unspoken words and late replies. Victoria set her phone aside. The colours in her painting seemed dull. Her heart was no longer in it.

The Confrontation
⛈

The following day, as the sun began to set with its golden light, Lucifer finally mustered the courage to reach out again. He was met with a swift, almost impatient reply — Victoria's worry finally breaking the surface.

The words they'd both been holding back…

VictoriaLuc, I get that you're busy — but can you at least let me know what's going on? I've been really worried.
LuciferI'm sorry, Victoria. I didn't mean to worry you. Things have just been really tough on my end.
VictoriaTough enough to not even reply for hours? I thought we were better than this. It feels like you don't care anymore.
LuciferIt's not that I don't care. I've just been overwhelmed. I'm really sorry if I made you feel that way.
VictoriaI just don't understand why you couldn't tell me that! I've been here — waiting, worrying.
LuciferI didn't want to burden you with my problems.
VictoriaWe're friends, Luc! It's not a burden. I want to be there for you — but I can't if you keep pushing me away.
After the Rain
🌤

The rain had finally stopped, leaving Aurora Vista refreshed and renewed. Lucifer stood on his balcony, staring out at the clear evening sky. He knew he had to make things right — to rekindle the bond that had meant so much to him.

Finding their way back

One message at a time, the warmth returned.

LuciferHey, V. I've been thinking a lot, and I want to make it up to you. Can we start over? I miss our talks and your laugh.
VictoriaI miss you too, Luc. Let's not let this happen again. We're too good at this friendship thing to let it fall apart.
VictoriaI finally finished that painting. You were right — the inspiration was just waiting to hit. It's probably my best work yet.
LuciferI knew you'd nail it! Can't wait to see it. Maybe your muse can give me a nudge with my coding blocks 😄
VictoriaConsider your muse on the way. Let's not let a few rough days keep us from what we have. Deal?
LuciferDeal. Here's to better days — and never letting a little silence get in the way again. ✨
Their friendship, once clouded by silence and uncertainty, was stronger than ever. The late replies and misunderstandings were behind them, replaced by a renewed commitment to be there for each other — no matter what.
— A Sudden Silence · The Distance Between Messages
