An Evening Written in Stars
Aurora Vista & Evervale — across the sky, across the screen
I
Aurora Vista · Evening The Storm

In the evening, there were minimal changes in the environment when an unexpected weather report was issued. It was very dark — the evening gave the impression that an evil god was entering the Earth with armies to fight someone. The sudden change was a little bit scary for everyone.

📍 Aurora Vista · Atmospheric Conditions

The cool air touched the trees, causing it to circulate into numerous tornados. The children of Aurora Vista came out of their houses, running and screaming through the streets — their voices loud with the exhilaration of weather made dramatic.

Lucifer took a picture and sent it to Victoria in the online forum: "Maybe it is the last day of Aurora Vista" — accompanied by a string of funny emojis.

Lucifer · Aurora Vista 📸 Evening walk through the dramatic storm — retreating home when the weather turned. Texted Victoria immediately to play online.
Victoria · Evervale 🌙 Replied with a photo of Evervale — cloudy, dark, moon visible. "Is this really your hometown?"

The landscapes of Aurora Vista looked even more beautiful in the storm. After seeing Victoria's image of Evervale, Lucifer replied: "It looks very cool!" — then asked, "Do you have time to play the game?" Victoria replied, "Hold your horses; I'll be there in a few minutes."

IV
Victoria's Window · Evervale · Night The Shooting Star

Lucifer and Victoria hastily cleaned up before retreating to their respective beds. Lucifer sprawled out with his mobile, catching up on forum discussions and light-hearted cartoons. Meanwhile, Victoria made herself comfortable, gazing out at the breathtaking scenery — savouring the refreshing breeze sweeping through the room.

As she looked up at the night sky, she watched as passing clouds obscured the moon and stars, some of which glimmered with a myriad of colours. Suddenly, her attention was captivated by a star streaking across the sky — its radiant glow captivating her until it disappeared beyond the horizon.

✦ The Moment — Victoria's Window · Evervale
"It was magical! I was just gazing up, lost in thought, when out of nowhere, this brilliant streak of light shot across the sky. It was like the universe was painting a dazzling line of light on its dark canvas — and then, poof, it was gone. Just like that!"

✦ In the Quiet After

In the depths of the night, as the world around them grew quiet, Lucifer and Victoria — possessed of pure souls — found themselves drawn into a private realm of introspection. Unbeknownst to each other, they pursued conversations with invisible companions, caring for themselves in solitary moments of profound self-reflection, akin to a series of poignant monologues.

From a storm-dark sky to a single falling star — one evening proved that even across the distance, some moments are shared by two souls at once.
