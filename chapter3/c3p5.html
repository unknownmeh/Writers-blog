Now · A New Dawn
🌅

Another day begins
with familiar warmth

Another day in their lives began with a beautiful dawn and the sounds of birds experiencing the wonders of nature. Lucifer from Aurora Vista and Victoria from Evervale started the day by texting each other — "Hola, amigos." They chatted while preparing their morning coffee and continued until the last sip.

☀ Morning Exchange
Lucifer
Hola, amigos ☕ How's the morning treating you over in Evervale?
Victoria
Hola! Perfect sunrise here. Coffee's brewing. You?
Lucifer
Same. Aurora Vista looks magical this morning — fresh snow on everything.
Victoria
Hey Lucifer, I will catch up later, gotta go. Enjoy the day 🌿
Lucifer
You too, Victoria. 🌄

Lucifer deeply values the companionship of his long-distance friend. Despite the rarity of their face-to-face encounters, their strong bond keeps them connected emotionally and mentally. He treasures every exchange of texts throughout the day — the prospect of Victoria not responding feels like an unsettling farewell he cannot bear.

The Plan · Aurora Vista Trip
🗺

Planning an adventure
in the snow

As dusk settled in Evervale, Lucifer proposed the idea of another adventure — this time in his hometown of Aurora Vista. The mere mention of it ignited immediate enthusiasm in Victoria, who had long been captivated by his vivid descriptions. They agreed the upcoming weekend would be ideal, with clear skies and just the right snowmelt to unveil the frozen waterfalls.

☕
The Valley Café
A charming café nestled amidst the valley, offering breathtaking panoramic views — perfect for steaming beverages and quiet conversation.
🏔
Waterfall Hike
A thrilling hike to the magnificent frozen waterfalls — an intimate encounter that would undoubtedly be a highlight of the visit.
🎨
Victoria's Sketchbook
Feeling creatively blocked, Victoria eagerly anticipated drawing inspiration from Aurora Vista's enchanting landscapes with her sketchbook in hand.
🖼
Local Art Gallery
A gallery showcasing works inspired by the landscape — Lucifer's hope to ignite the spark of creativity within his artistic friend.
🍕
Spicy Chicken Pizza
Their shared love for spicy chicken pizza — they eagerly decided to make it together, cooking and savouring a meal side by side.
💎
Crystal Caves
Near Lucifer's apartment — light dancing off glistening crystals, enveloping the space in a mesmerising, almost otherworldly ambiance.
"
Their upcoming adventure in Aurora Vista would be more than just a trip — it would be a chance to deepen their bond, create new memories, and explore the world through each other's eyes.
— An Evening's Promise
Flashback Three Years Ago…
The Solo Journey · Evervale → Aurora Vista
🚌

A night bus, a
guiding voice from afar

Three years ago, Lucifer embarked on a bus journey from Evervale without Victoria by his side — yet she was never truly absent. Through an online forum, she stayed connected, providing continual support and concern for his well-being every step of the way.

🚌 Night Route
Evervale
🚌
Aurora Vista
Departed at Dusk · Arrived at 11:30 PM · Journey: ~6 hours
Dusk
🌇
The Bus Stop Farewell
Evervale · Departure
The sun gracefully descended beyond the horizon, saturating Evervale in a warm golden hue. Lucifer stood contemplatively at the bus stop, poised for his first ever solo journey — a profound personal milestone.
Victoria · Message Hope you've packed some snacks and a charger! The ride can be long and tiring.
Evening
🌙
Into the Countryside
Rolling hills · Moonlit roads
The bus smoothly traversed the winding route, its rhythmic sway lulling passengers into a peaceful slumber. Lucifer remained alert, clutching his phone and diligently monitoring Victoria's continuous messages. Outside, the scenery transformed from bustling town to serene, moonlit countryside.
Victoria · Message You should visit the café at Maple Street when you switch buses. Their hot chocolate is simply divine.
Mid-Journey
🔋
The Battery Scare
Low power · High anxiety
The snowfall intensified, covering the landscape in a delicate veil of white. With every passing minute, Lucifer's phone battery depleted — causing him to worry about losing his vital connection to Victoria.
Victoria · Message Don't panic! There's a charging station at your next stop. Plug in and grab a bite; you'll need your energy for the rest of the trip.
Night
☕
The Transfer Stop
A dimly lit station · Hot chocolate
At a small, dimly lit station, Lucifer disembarked to switch buses — the station enveloped in profound silence. He plugged in his phone and indulged in a steaming hot chocolate from the café. With every comforting sip, warmth spread through his body.
Late Night
🌨
The Final Leg
Snow-covered hills · Almost home
The bus was sparsely populated, affording Lucifer an entire row to himself. As he gazed out at the serene snowy landscape, a profound sense of tranquillity and eager anticipation washed over him.
Victoria · Message You're almost there! Make sure to get off at Maple Street. Bundle up — it's going to be cold!
11:30 PM
Arrived · Aurora Vista · Safe & Sound

The small village appeared tranquil, covered in a serene blanket of snow sparkling in the moonlight. The only sound: the muffled crunch of snow under his boots as he made his way to his apartment.

🌙 Last Messages of the Night
Lucifer
I'm home safe and sound, thanks to you. Couldn't have done it without your help tonight.
Victoria
So glad you're home! Get some rest; you deserve it after such an adventure. Can't wait to hear all about it tomorrow. 🌙
Back to present Now…
The Wait · Present Day
✨

Waiting for the
moment to arrive

Lucifer came back to reality, thinking about Victoria — excited to meet his friend and waiting for the moment. This time, it wouldn't be a solo journey. This time, she would be there beside him in Aurora Vista, where the frozen waterfalls waited, the crystal caves shimmered, and the valley café sat ready for two.

"
Despite being miles apart, her presence had been a guiding light — making his solo journey feel less daunting and more like a shared adventure.
— An Evening's Promise
