🌅 Early Morning
☕ Lucifer · Aurora Vista A Fresh Dawn Groggy and warm, sitting at his kitchen table with a laptop open and coffee in hand, catching up on the morning's messages.
🎨 Victoria · Evervale Sketching by the Window The early morning sun casting a glow on her sketchbook, birds chirping outside, mid-doodle on a new painting idea.

It was a fresh morning in Aurora Vista, and the first light of dawn filtered through the curtains of Lucifer's modest apartment. The scent of freshly brewed coffee filled the air, adding comforting warmth to the room. Meanwhile, in Evervale, Victoria was sitting by her window — the early morning sun casting a gentle glow on her sketchbook. She was in the middle of doodling a new idea for her next painting when her phone buzzed with a message from Lucifer.

Evervale Forums · Morning Online
Lucifer
Good morning, Victoria! How's the weather over in Evervale today?
Victoria
Morning, Lucifer! It's a bit cloudy here, but the birds are singing, so that's a good sign. How about Aurora Vista?
Lucifer
It's pretty calm here. Had a little rain last night, so the air is fresh. I'm just waking up with some coffee. You?
Victoria
I'm up early, sketching by the window. Just made some tea. What's on your agenda for today?
Lucifer
Gotta tackle a few projects at work. My boss has been piling on the tasks. 😅 How about you? Any new art projects?
Victoria
Yeah, actually! I'm working on a new piece inspired by last night's dream. It's a bit abstract, but I'm excited to see where it goes.
Lucifer
That sounds awesome! 😄
Victoria
Aww, thanks! And I wish I had your focus. I don't know how you manage to stay so organized at work. 🤔
Lucifer
Ha-ha, it's all about making to-do lists and sticking to them. You should try it sometime. 😉
Victoria
I might just take you up on that. Oh, and guess what? I found this new café in town. They have the best pastries! We should check it out next time you visit.
Lucifer
Sounds like a plan! I'm always up for good pastries. 😋
Victoria
Great! Let's set a date soon. Anyway, I should get back to my sketch before the inspiration fades. Have a good day at work, Lucifer!
Lucifer
You too, Vicky! Have fun with your sketching. Talk to you later!
Victoria
Will do! Bye! 👋
☕
📅 A Plan in the Making

"I found this new café in town — they have the best pastries! We should check it out next time you visit." A small, warm promise tucked into a morning chat. The kind that makes a future visit feel already real.

🌤️ Around Noon

🌤 Noon · Aurora Vista Office

Lucifer's office had large windows letting in plenty of natural light. In the break room — a cosy space with a few tables, a fridge, and a microwave — the smell of various lunch dishes being heated filled the air, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere.

🏢
🍝 Lucifer's Lunch Spaghetti Marinara Leftover spaghetti with marinara, mushrooms, and a dusting of parmesan. Simple, comfort food — his go-to.
🥪 Victoria's Lunch Turkey & Avocado Whole grain bread, turkey, avocado, crispy lettuce and tomatoes. Fresh and full of flavour — just her style.
Evervale Forums · Noon Online
Lucifer
Hey Victoria, how's your lunch break going?
Victoria
Hey Luci! It's good, just grabbed a sandwich at my favourite café. You?
Lucifer
Nice! I'm having some leftover pasta I made last night. It's pretty simple but tasty. What kind of sandwich did you get?
Victoria
I got a turkey and avocado sandwich with some crispy lettuce and tomatoes. It's on whole grain bread. I love how fresh everything tastes here.
Lucifer
That sounds delicious! I've always liked turkey sandwiches, especially when they have avocado. It adds such a nice creamy texture.
Victoria
Exactly! Avocado makes everything better. What kind of pasta are you having?
Lucifer
It's spaghetti with marinara sauce. I added some mushrooms and a bit of parmesan on top. Nothing fancy, but it's my go-to comfort food.
Victoria
Yum, I love spaghetti! Mushrooms add such a nice earthy flavour. Do you cook a lot?
Lucifer
I try to. I find it relaxing after a long day at work. Plus, it's fun to experiment with different recipes. What about you? Do you like cooking?
Victoria
I do, but I'm more of a baker. I love making cakes and cookies. There's something really satisfying about mixing ingredients and watching them turn into something sweet and delicious.
Lucifer
Baking is awesome! I'm not much of a baker myself, but I do enjoy eating baked goods. 😄 Do you have a favourite recipe?
Victoria
Probably my chocolate chip cookies. They're always a hit with my friends. What's your favourite dish to cook?
Lucifer
I think my favourite is a good old-fashioned Spicy Noodles. It takes a bit of time to make, but it's always worth it — what's not to love?
Victoria
Spicy Noodles is amazing! I haven't made it in a while, but now you're making me crave it. Maybe I'll give it a try this weekend.
Lucifer
You should! By the way, do you ever try out new cafés or restaurants in Evervale?
Victoria
All the time! There's this new place that just opened up, and they have the best homemade soups. I tried their tomato basil soup last week — it was heavenly.
Lucifer
That sounds great. We should meet up for lunch sometime and check out a new place together.
Victoria
Definitely! It would be so much fun to explore new food spots with you. Maybe we can find a place that serves amazing desserts too.
Lucifer
Now you're talking my language! I'm always up for good food and great company. Let's make it happen soon.
Victoria
For sure! Anyway, I should finish up my lunch and get back to my art project. Have a good rest of your workday, Luci!
Lucifer
You too, Vicky! Enjoy your sandwich and have fun with your project. Talk to you later! 😄
🍝 Lucifer's Favourite Dish

Spicy Noodles — "It takes a bit of time to make, but it's always worth it. What's not to love?" A dish that takes patience and gives comfort in return. Much like the friendship itself.

🍪 Victoria's Signature Recipe

Chocolate Chip Cookies — always a hit with her friends. There's something really satisfying about mixing ingredients and watching them turn into something sweet and delicious.

✦ The Heart of It

Lucifer and Victoria share a simple, friendly chat about their lunch choices and favourite dishes — their cooking habits, their food dreams, their plans to one day sit at the same table. Their conversation is light-hearted and casual, reflecting their close friendship and their shared enjoyment of the good, quiet things in life.
