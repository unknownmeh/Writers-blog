What Victoria
Felt

// victoria's inner world //
A Bond Worth Everything

Victoria couldn't help but feel a wave of warmth as she reflected on her friendship with Lucifer. It wasn't just about the brushes — it was about the thoughtfulness behind them.

Lucifer had a way of understanding her needs, often before she even voiced them. He had this uncanny ability to sense when she was struggling. Whether she was feeling stuck with her work or just needed a little boost of confidence, he always seemed to know exactly what to do or say to lift her spirits.

In a world where people often looked out for their own interests, Lucifer's selflessness stood out. She was truly happy to have someone like him in her life — a friend who not only understood her but also made her feel seen, heard, and supported.

"In her heart, she knew that their friendship was something special — something to be treasured."

— Victoria's Reflection · Chapter IV
